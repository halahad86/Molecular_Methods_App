Molecular Methods Introduction


Molecular Methods is a course designed to allow you to develop practical skills and understanding in molecular biology. To accomplish this there are a number of linked experiments that you will perform to take you through a process from PCR to gene cloning, plasmid DNA preparation, DNA sequencing and sequence analysis. The labs will build knowledge and confidence, allowing you to plan and perform your own experiments in the future!
